A

Gradient column headers + left-accent cards

Matches the app's existing gradient language (PageHeader, MetricCards). Bold, premium. The left border on cards gives instant status recognition without changing the card structure.

B

Tinted column backgrounds + tinted cards

Softer, all-over colour wash. Feels more like a document tool (Notion/Linear). Colour-coded but less bold than gradients.

C

Gradient metric bar + top-strip cards

Brings the dashboard's gradient MetricCards energy into the kanban header. Clean white columns. Cards have a top gradient strip as the status signal.
